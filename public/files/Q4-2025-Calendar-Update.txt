Q4 2025 Treasury Issuance Calendar Update
==========================================
Updated: November 10, 2025

The Bank of Ghana has updated the Treasury issuance calendar for Q4 2025.

Updated Auction Schedule:
- 91-Day Treasury Bill: Weekly auctions
- 182-Day Treasury Bill: Bi-weekly auctions  
- 364-Day Treasury Bill: Monthly auctions
- 2-Year Government Bond: Quarterly auctions

Additional auction dates have been added to meet increased demand.
Target amounts have been adjusted based on market conditions.

For complete schedule, visit www.bog.gov.gh
